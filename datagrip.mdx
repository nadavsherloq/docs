## Don't find your IDE here? 
We're actively working on adding more integrations, prioritizing them based on our users' requests.<br></br> **Let us know here what you're using**, and in the meantime, feel free to use our Chrome extension and set it as your default page.

### Directly from your IDE, you can:
1. Save queries / console 
2. Share queries in one click
3. Inject snippets code using keyboard shorcut 

And.. much more! Look out for the [Product Features](/features) to explore more about Sherloq capabilities.
