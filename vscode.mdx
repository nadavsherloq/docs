# Option 2: Install via Jetbrains Settings (Marketplace)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open DataGrip Settings">
    Windows: `ctrl` + `alt` + `s`

    Mac: `⌘` + `,`

    <img alt="Sherloq Banner" height="150" width="150" src="/images/datagripplugin-setting.png" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click 'Plugins' on the left side and search for 'Sherloq'">
    <img alt="Sherloq Banner" height="250" width="250" src="/images/datagripplugin-install-plugin.png" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click install & Restart your IDE">
    <img alt="Sherloq Banner" height="200" width="220" src="/images/datagripplugin-restart-ide.png" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Congratulations 🚀" icon="party-horn">
    You have installed Sherloq in your Datagrip!
  </Step>
</Steps>
