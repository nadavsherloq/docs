In the settings, you can choose your preferred SQL dialect.
Once applied, your selected dialect and formatting will be saved accordingly.

## Formatting directly in your editor

Highlight your SQL, right-click, or use `Cmd/Ctrl + Shift + 0` to format it instantly in your editor.

## Fomratting in Sherloq

To easily read a query as you’re used to in your IDE, in the `query profile` click on the formatting button to adjust the indentation and spacing automatically.
