---
title: 'Versions Notes'
---

To add a note to each version, click on the notes icon. This will open the shared notes for that version. 

Make sure to save your changes after updating the notes. 
<br></br>If the notes box is not empty, a red notification will appear on the icon to indicate that someone has left a note for this version.
