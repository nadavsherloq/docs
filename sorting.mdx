title: 'Sorting'
---

Our search offers two sorting options:

- **Relevance**
- **Last created**

We’re working hard to deliver the best search results, which is why we offer the "Relevance" sorting option.

 This is based on a combination of criteria, including the number of activities and other custom properties (for example, queries you created will appear with higher priority).
